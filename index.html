About

Hello and welcome to my homepage!

I am a final-year PhD student in Physics at the Heidelberg Institute of Theoretical Studies and fellow of the International Max Planck Research School for Astronomy and Cosmic Physics. My research interests are stellar evolution, core collapse supernovae and binary neutron star merger.

You can reach out to me via mail: kiril.maltsev@h-its.org
